Overview of Mega Jaya

Mega Jaya Security Sdn. Bhd. (1089580-M) is a wholly owned private limited Bumiputera company incorporated under the Private Agency Act 1971. The company specializes in delivering private and professional security services to various industries across Malaysia.

Mega Jaya is dedicated to protecting clients' investments and properties. The company emphasizes prevention over cure, offering services designed to relieve clients from security worries and provide peace of mind. Mega Jaya aims to deliver high-quality and reliable security services through skilled, professional personnel, while uplifting Malaysia’s security standards, introducing improved security practices, and supporting national development through greater employment opportunities.
